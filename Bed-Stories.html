1. The Tears of a Bed

am a bed!

Where would you have found comfort if I didn’t exist?

i make you feel home when you’re tired

Tired from the trials and tribulations of this earth

The earth that favours had workers

Ironically politicians are also hard workers

Hard workers that prioritize tax

Tax of poor souls goes into the lake

Like a narrow river murmuring down into the lake

To change its colour into sky blue and make it attractive to human fresh

Am still the same bed

That let you sleep on me without giving you pressure like the way your government do

i let you nil down or hold me and talk to the creator of the universe

Have you ever think of me?

Am a bed

My life is like human development

When years pass, you easily replaces me

i understand you’re my master

Am a bed.
