1. Developers Tribute

On that 13th of August 2004

Straight from home

You went to work

Past of a fateful loof

You fell down and collapsed

Death, untimely, death

All dreams dumbstruck

Some fingers pointed at witches

Others at the creator

We remained with unanswered questions

Fair thee well

Daveson Rueben Kondowe
