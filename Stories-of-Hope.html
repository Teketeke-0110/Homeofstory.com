1. The World Will Adjust

Because every picture tells a story

A hidden story with great memories

Memories that put a smile on your face

Memories that can draw down tears

Tears of hope and regret

Far you have come

The future looks promising

Your scars are a reminder that you survived your deepest wounds

A good lesson to keep you going

Never give up

A tree do not grow in a day

But with time it bears fruits

The world will adjust to fit your desires

Patience is the key

Life is journey

Your life is moving according to the plot and story devised by the one above
