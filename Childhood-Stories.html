1. When We Were Young

When we were young

So young, innocent and energetic

Not knowing what the future holds

No worries of where to get food

No worries about the economy

No worries about the self-development

When we were young

Playing is all that was in our mind

No worry of who to vote for

No worry about taking a bath

No worry of what to wear

When we were young

We shared what we had with others

Even if we did not know them

When we were young

We travelled together to unknown places

We returned home together safe and sound

When we were young

We did not know racism

Everyone was cool like ice

Everything start with a dream and vision.
