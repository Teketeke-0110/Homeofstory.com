1. Kelvin and Trizar

Once upon a time in 2019,

In the green city of Zomba

Existed a beautiful and nice couple

Kelvin and Trizar

They planned a road trip to Brantyre city, Malawi

The sun set, morning came

With excitement,

Kelvin twitched on the ignition

The engine pistons hinted TDC and BDC

They started off their journey at 8am CAT in Zomba

With smiles on their faces,

They looked at each other

At 8:30am they approached Thondwe bakery stop over

A popular trading centre in the district

They stopped by and have some bottles of beers

Kelvin took six bottles of Carlsberg chill,

While Trizar took three shots of Malawi Brandy for quick boost

She then took a six pack of Hunters Gold

They agreed to proceed their journey

Trizar took over the driving

Kelvin became the DJ

He played Black Coffee drive me crazy

Both singed along to the song joyfully

At 11:30am CAT,

They were in Blantyre

They proceeded straight to Amaryllis Hotel

And parked their BMW M3 at B2

And jumped into the elevator

Kelvin pressed B4 “destination”

With beautiful scenery view,

They sat at B4 admiring the beauty of Blantyre city

Whilst waiting for the ordered Pizza,

Kelvin kissed Trizar on her beautiful forehead

They went to B3 to book a room

And spent a night together

Morning came.
